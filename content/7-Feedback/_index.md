### Overall Evaluation

**1. Working Environment**  
The working environment was friendly, welcoming, and open. The mentors and members of the First Cloud AI Journey (FCAJ) program were always willing to provide guidance, share their experience, and support me throughout my internship. In addition, networking and community activities gave me the opportunity to build meaningful connections, learn from people with similar interests, and quickly adapt to the workplace. Overall, it was a positive and inspiring environment that encouraged both my professional and personal development.

**2. Support from Mentor / Team Admin**  
The most memorable part of my internship was the dedication of the mentors and the admin team. Even though there were a large number of participants nearly 800 students from HUTECH alone, not including students from many other universities they were always willing to answer questions and provide support whenever I needed help. Whether the questions were technical, academic, or even very basic, they always responded patiently and made everyone feel comfortable asking for assistance.

What I appreciated most was that they did not simply provide answers. Instead, they encouraged us to think independently and try solving problems on our own before guiding us in the right direction and filling in the missing knowledge. This approach not only helped me solve immediate challenges but also strengthened my self learning ability and confidence when facing new problems. Their kindness, encouragement, and willingness to support every student made my internship experience both meaningful and unforgettable.

**3. Relevance of Work to Academic Major**  
Although my major is Software Engineering, the tasks assigned during my internship were closely related to the knowledge I had learned at university, including programming, system design, application development, and the software development lifecycle. At the same time, I had the opportunity to explore a new field Cloud Computing through various AWS services. Since cloud technology was completely new to me at the beginning of the internship, I gradually became familiar with it by studying, practicing, and working on a real-world project. This experience not only strengthened my software engineering foundation but also broadened my technical expertise and equipped me with valuable skills for my future career.

**4. Learning & Skill Development Opportunities**  
Throughout my internship, I had the opportunity to develop both my technical knowledge and soft skills. In addition to learning AWS services and working on a real-world project, I also attended Saturday events where I gained valuable insights into how businesses operate, professional working practices, and problem-solving approaches. I also improved my teamwork, communication, task management, and problem-solving skills. Most importantly, the mentors' and guest speakers' experiences, career advice, and learning strategies gave me a clearer vision of my future career path.

**5. Company Culture & Team Spirit**  
What impressed me the most was the open, positive, and supportive working culture. The mentors, admin team, and members of the program were always willing to share their knowledge, help others overcome challenges, and encourage everyone to learn and grow together.

**6. Internship Policies / Benefits**  
One of the greatest advantages of the internship program was its flexibility. Depending on approval, I could work from the office or remotely from home, allowing me to better manage my schedule. In addition, Saturday events featuring guest speakers, mentors, and alumni of the program provided valuable opportunities to learn from their real-world experiences and career journeys. These sharing sessions were truly inspiring and became one of the most meaningful parts of my internship experience.

### Additional Questions
- What did you find **most satisfying** during your internship?  

  What satisfied me the most was seeing my own growth throughout the internship. I started as a student with almost no practical knowledge of AWS, but step by step I learned, overcame the initial challenges, and eventually became capable of contributing to the development and deployment of a real-world cloud project. Throughout the internship, I was grateful for the opportunity to gain new knowledge, strengthen my technical and professional skills, and develop a deeper understanding of how technology is applied in a real working environment.
  
- What do you think the company **should improve** for future interns?  
  
  In my opinion, the program is already very well organized and provides great value to students. If possible, I would recommend organizing more online events so that interns can participate more easily and continue learning regardless of their location. In addition, introducing mini games or small challenges during each event would create a more engaging atmosphere, increase interaction, and provide more opportunities for interns to connect, exchange ideas, and learn from one another.

- If recommending to a friend, would you **suggest they intern here**? Why or why not?
 
  Absolutely. I believe this is one of the most worthwhile programs to join. It not only helps students build a strong foundation of knowledge but also provides an environment where they can practice, make mistakes, learn from experience, and grow alongside others who share the same passion. It is an excellent opportunity to prepare for a professional working environment and build a solid foundation for a future career.

### Suggestions & Expectations
- Do you have any suggestions to improve the internship experience?

  I hope the program can organize more regular online workshops and events so that interns have more opportunities to update their knowledge, share experiences, and connect with one another. In addition, incorporating mini games or interactive activities into each event would create a more engaging atmosphere, motivate participants to learn, and strengthen the sense of community among interns.

- Would you like to continue this program in the future?  
  
  Yes. I would be delighted to continue being part of the First Cloud AI Journey after completing my internship. As I gain more experience, I also hope to have the opportunity to support and share my knowledge with future interns, just as the mentors guided and inspired me throughout this journey.

- Any other comments (free sharing):
  
  I am truly grateful for the opportunity to be a part of the First Cloud AI Journey. The program not only introduced me to AWS and Cloud Computing but also helped me grow in terms of technical knowledge, professional skills, and career orientation. Starting with almost no practical experience, I gradually gained the confidence to contribute to the development and deployment of a real-world project. This will always remain one of the most meaningful and memorable experiences of my student life. I would like to express my sincere gratitude to the organizing committee, the mentors, and the admin team for their continuous support, encouragement, and inspiration throughout this journey.
