### Progress Achieved

When I first started my internship, I had almost no practical experience with AWS and had never deployed a system on a cloud platform. I was unfamiliar with AWS services, the software development lifecycle, and the professional working environment. My learning journey began with the fundamentals, and I gradually built my knowledge through self-study, official documentation, and hands-on experience with a real-world project.Throughout the internship, I became familiar with various AWS services, learned how to build and deploy applications using a Serverless architecture, and improved my skills in programming, system deployment, teamwork, and problem-solving. Every task assigned to me helped me gain valuable experience, increased my confidence, and strengthened my ability to adapt to a professional working environment.By the end of the internship, I had progressed from a student with almost no AWS knowledge to someone capable of contributing to the development, deployment, and testing of the SmartMenu project on AWS. I also gained a better understanding of the complete software development and deployment process. This internship marked an important milestone in my professional growth and provided a solid foundation for pursuing a career in Cloud Computing.However, I also recognize that my current knowledge and experience are only the beginning. There are still many advanced AWS services, cloud technologies, and professional skills that I need to learn and master. These limitations motivate me to continue improving myself, expand my technical expertise, and prepare for the challenges of a professional career in the IT industry.

### Needs Improvement

Besides the knowledge and experience I gained during my internship, I recognize that there are still several areas where I need to improve in order to better meet the requirements of a professional working environment. Although I always strive to complete my assigned tasks, there are times when I am not proactive enough in managing my time and organizing my work, which affects my overall productivity. In addition, I need to further strengthen my analytical thinking and problem-solving skills so that I can identify root causes more quickly and develop appropriate solutions when unexpected situations arise. Furthermore, my knowledge of AWS is currently focused mainly on the technologies used in the internship project, so I plan to continue studying more advanced AWS services and architectures to broaden my expertise. Going forward, I will maintain a strong commitment to self-learning, stay up to date with emerging technologies, and continue developing my discipline, logical thinking, and professional skills to better prepare myself for future career opportunities.
