# FCAJ Community Day 23/5/2026

### Event Objectives

- Update knowledge and new trends about AI and AWS.
- Introduce AWS technologies and services such as Amazon Q and Amazon CloudFront along with practical applications.
- Share experience in product development, participating in hackathons, and deploying real projects.
- Improve understanding of how LLM models and Multi-Agent AI systems operate in enterprise environments.

### Speakers

- **Tinh Truong** - Platform Engineer, GoTymeX
- **Anh Pham** - Cloud Consultant, G-AsiaPacific Vietnam
- **Thinh Nguyen** - DevOps Engineer, FCAJ
- **Team UTMorpho** (Uyen Le, Thao Nguyen, Mai Nguyen) - GenAI Engineers, VIB
- **Duc Dao** - Solutions Architect, Cloud Kinetics
- **Vy Lam** - Senior Business Systems Analyst, VPBank

### Key Highlights

- **In-depth AI & AWS Knowledge Sharing**: Practical experience in researching and deploying technology in enterprises.
- **Product Mindset**: From initial idea to deploying products in reality, along with real lessons learned from projects and Hackathon competitions.
- **Career Orientation**: Deep perspectives on career paths and necessary skills in the IT industry.

### Key Takeaways

- **Applying AI Models**: Deeper understanding of how to implement LLMs in practical applications.
- **Key Concept Clarification**: The role of Context, AI Memory, and MCP (Model Context Protocol) in enhancing the efficiency and reliability of AI systems.
- **LLM Reasoning Mechanism**: Deeper understanding of how LLMs process information, instability in generating results, and how to build Multi-Agent systems for complex tasks.
- **Hackathon Experience**: How to build a complete product in just 18 hours by optimizing product mindset, smooth teamwork, effective time management, and leveraging available tech tools.

### Event Experience

Attending the **"FCAJ Community Day 23/5/2026"** on the 26th Floor of Bitexco Financial Tower was a highly beneficial and meaningful experience. Besides updating state-of-the-art tech knowledge from leading experts (Platform Engineers, Solutions Architects, GenAI Engineers), I also had the opportunity to network and learn from experienced professionals about real-world practices in large enterprises. The Hackathon shares inspired me greatly and boosted my motivation to learn.
